Who Am I

Hello, I'm Ismayle Santos...

Professor at Ceará State University (UECE) and member of both GESAD and GREat. His main interests are: intelligent software engineering, software testing, computer science education, and industry-academia collaborations. He was Visiting Professor at the Computer Science Department/UFC from 2020 to 2022 and Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Informatics (INF)/UFRGS from 2018 to 2020. He holds a doctoral degree (2017) and a master's degree (2013) in Computer Science from Federal University of Ceará. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree (2010) with honours in Computer Science from Federal University of Piauí. He receives the second best master's thesis award (2014) in the Brazilian Symposium on Software Quality 's Master Dissertation Contest, and the best Ph.D. thesis award (2018) in the Brazilian Symposium on Software Quality 's PhD Thesis Contest. He also have more than 10 years' experience in R&D projects through Industry-Academia Collaborations. In addition, he is the co-founder of goTest, a UX and Software Testing startup.
